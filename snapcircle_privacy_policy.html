SnapCircle Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for the SnapCircle mobile application.

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

1. Introduction

SnapCircle is a social photo-sharing application that allows users to create or join private circles, start live snap activities, upload photos, view shared moments, interact through comments and reactions, and revisit past live snap sessions.

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapCircle collects, uses, stores, and protects user information. By using SnapCircle, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use SnapCircle:

  • Account information: such as your name, email address, profile details, and authentication information.
  • Circle information: such as circles you create or join, member lists, invitations, and participation status.
  • Photo content: photos uploaded by users during live snap activities or shared moments.
  • Interaction data: comments, likes, reactions, activity history, and live snap participation records.
  • Device information: device identifiers, app version, operating system, and Firebase Cloud Messaging token for notifications.
  • Usage information: basic app activity used to improve performance, stability, and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Enable users to create, join, and manage circles.
  • Allow users to start and participate in live snap activities.
  • Store and display uploaded photos, comments, reactions, timelines, photo walls, feeds, and histories.
  • Send notifications related to live snap activities, reminders, invitations, and app updates.
  • Improve app performance, security, reliability, and user experience.
  • Prevent misuse, unauthorized access, spam, and abusive behavior.

4. Photos and User Content

SnapCircle allows users to upload photos and share them within their circles or feeds based on app functionality. Users are responsible for the content they upload and share.

Uploaded content may be visible to members of the relevant circle or to areas of the app where the user chooses to share that content.

5. Notifications

SnapCircle may use push notifications to inform users about live snap activities, reminders, circle updates, invitations, and participation alerts. You can manage notification permissions from your device settings.

6. Firebase and Third-Party Services

SnapCircle may use Google Firebase services for authentication, database storage, cloud storage, notifications, analytics, crash reporting, and app security. These services may process certain user, device, and usage data according to their own privacy and security practices.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With service providers required to operate the app, such as Firebase and cloud infrastructure services.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of SnapCircle, its users, or the public.

8. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

We retain user information and uploaded content for as long as necessary to provide SnapCircle services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Users may request deletion of their account or data by contacting us at the email address provided below.

10. Children’s Privacy

SnapCircle is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. User Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also request account deletion or data removal by contacting us.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of SnapCircle after changes means you accept the updated policy.

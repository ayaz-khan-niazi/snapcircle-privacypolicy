SnapCircle Child Safety Standards

SnapCircle has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, trafficking, sextortion, or any content or behavior that harms or exploits minors.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

SnapCircle does not allow:

  • Sexual content involving minors.
  • Any request, attempt, or encouragement to create, share, store, or distribute CSAM.
  • Grooming, predatory behavior, sexual solicitation, or coercion involving minors.
  • Nude, sexual, exploitative, or abusive images of minors.
  • Harassment, threats, blackmail, or sextortion.
  • Any use of SnapCircle to endanger children or facilitate abuse.

Reporting

Users can report safety concerns, abusive behavior, or suspected CSAE by contacting us at: sundar.niazi1@gmail.com

Reports involving child safety are prioritized and reviewed as quickly as possible.

Enforcement

If we identify CSAE, CSAM, grooming, exploitation, or other child safety violations, we may:

  • Remove the content.
  • Suspend or terminate the account.
  • Restrict access to groups or app features.
  • Preserve relevant information where legally required.
  • Report suspected child sexual abuse material or exploitation to appropriate authorities, including NCMEC or local law enforcement where applicable.

Prevention

SnapCircle is designed for private group photo sharing. We encourage users to only join groups with people they know and trust. Users should not share sensitive, sexual, abusive, or exploitative content.

Compliance

SnapCircle complies with applicable child safety laws and Google Play child safety policies. We cooperate with lawful requests from authorities when required.
